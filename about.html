About Diana

My Story

I can be your developer or designer with my experience creating web pages and using design tools. I believe in meaningful design to solve real-life problems and create a seamless user experience. I hope that my work can make a positive impact on people and the company I work for.

I earned a degree in informatics and focused on user experience with my concentration in human-computer interaction (HCI). After college, I worked as an analyst at a Big Four technology company.

I went back to school for about a year to earn certificates in design and frontend devlopment. I learned how to create beautiful user interfaces while helping users have a positive, effortless experience with technology.
