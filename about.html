About Diana

My Story

I am a designer and frontend developer that believes in meaningful design to solve real-life problems. My favorite things about design are the creativity and collaboration involved to create a seamless user experience.

I started college thinking I wanted to be in health care. As I transitioned into technology by letting go of pre-med and studying informatics, I still found a strong desire to help people. I focused on user experience with my concentration in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). I hope that my work can make a positive impact on people and the company I work for.

I moved to Austin, Texas after college and worked as an analyst for six months.

I chose to go back to school and learn design because it allows me to create beautiful user interfaces and help users have a positive, effortless experience with technology. When I am not creating, I enjoy traveling, trying new food, hiking, and spending time with my dog. My favorite places to travel are the national and state parks in the United States. I love a good view and fresh air away from the city!
