Overview:

Tune Out is a pixel-perfect design of a music player app for people who like music. The design process included utilizing grids and annotation with all elements made ready to hand off to developers.

Purpose:

The purpose of this project was to apply design patterns to a music player app after analyzing the design of competing music player apps. This was my first project for my CareerFoundry course.

Role & Duration:

1 month

  • UI Designer

Tools:

  • Sketch
  • InVision

Objective:

Problem -  I found that some music player apps could be confusing to navigate, and I wanted my design to be as straightforward as possible.

Solution - My goal was to implement design patterns through key features in my music player app. I aimed for my app to be easy to navigate and user-friendly. Some key features I included in the app are navigation, input screens to log in and sign up, ways to like a song, follow artists, and view playlists.

Process:

I compared competing music player apps to gain inspiration on what design patterns I wanted to implement in my app.

I brainstormed elements and features by sketching low-fidelity wireframes.

Mid-fidelity wireframes were designed in Sketch. I implemented grids to provide a framework for my designs and included annotation for consistency.

I created a mood board to inspire the branding of my app. I found inspiration from things related to older music. I noticed the past few years people like vinyl records again, and I was largely inspired by them.

Music provides stress-relief and joy to a lot of people. That is why I chose Tune Out as the app name. The font for Tune Out fits the retro vibe of the app. The font for the rest of the app is simple and easy to read.  For the color palette, I picked colors that followed the vintage theme.

In the end, my designs were ready to be viewed on an iOS prototype using InVision. Since InVision has updated their plans to only one free prototype, I have moved this project to be viewed as a prototype in the Sketch cloud. Any elements and symbols in Sketch were made ready to be exported and available to pass on to developers.
