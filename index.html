Greener Life

Greener Life is an app designed for iOS and Android devices to help people live a more sustainable life through habit tracking.

My design process for this project included following the Human Interface Guidelines and Material Guidelines for device consistency, creating a mobile gesture guide, user testing, and device prototypes.
